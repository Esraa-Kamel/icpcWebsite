Ras Mohammed Nature Reserve is a true wonderland of natural beauty and marine diversity, situated at the very southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula. Here, visitors can indulge themselves in an aquatic paradise, with crystal-clear waters teeming with thousands of species of fish and coral reefs of every shape and color imaginable.
This nature reserve is a haven for scuba divers and snorkelers, with underwater vistas that rival those of the most celebrated dive spots in the world. From colorful clownfish to majestic turtles, from exotic lionfish to playful dolphins, every dive is a unique and unforgettable experience.
Above the water, the stunning cliffs of Ras Mohammed National Park tower over the Red Sea, providing a dazzling backdrop for hikers and nature lovers alike. The park's unique geology and diverse flora and fauna create a true wilderness that feels worlds away from civilization.
