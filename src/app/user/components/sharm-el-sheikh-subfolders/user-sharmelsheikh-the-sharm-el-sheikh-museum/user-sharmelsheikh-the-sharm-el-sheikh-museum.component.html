The Sharm El-Sheikh Museum provides visitors with a unique insight into the Sinai's rich history and culture. From ancient artefacts to contemporary artworks, the museum is a treasure trove of marvellous exhibits that will captivate visitors from all over the world.
As you enter the museum, you'll be greeted by stunning displays of ancient Egyptian artefacts dating back thousands of years, such as pottery, jewellery, and statuettes. You'll learn about the Bedouin people's ancient roots and unique way of life, including their traditional clothing, tools, and crafts.
