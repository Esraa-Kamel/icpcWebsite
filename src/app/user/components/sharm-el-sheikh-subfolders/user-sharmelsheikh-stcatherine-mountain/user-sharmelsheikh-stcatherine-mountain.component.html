St. Catherine Mountain is the site where Moses received the Ten Commandments from God, and since ancient times, it has been a place of pilgrimage for people of all faiths. The mountain is home to the stunning St. Catherine's Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that dates back to the 4th century AD and is one of the oldest working Christian monasteries in the world.
St. Catherine Mountain doesn't end with its rich history and mythology, it's also a place for adventurous travelers, the mountain offers a range of exciting activities, from hiking and rock climbing to camel riding and camping.
