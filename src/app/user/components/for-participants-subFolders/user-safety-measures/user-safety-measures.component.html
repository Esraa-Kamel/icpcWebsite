Health Measures:

  • ICPC hotels implement strict security measures, including metal detectors and hand baggage scans upon entering the hotel premises.
  • ICPC ensures participant security by organizing and supervising transportation, ensuring a secure and convenient experience for all.
  • Public access to ICPC events is restricted to registered guests.
  • ICPC hotels, and the ICPC Operations Team have solid emergency and evacuation plans in place to ensure the safety and well-being of everyone involved.

Security Measures:

  • ICPC hotels implement strict security measures, including metal detectors and hand baggage scans upon entering the hotel premises.
  • All transportation to and from the airport and between hotels and the contest venue is organized and controlled by ICPC/Host staff and Host volunteers.
  • Host volunteers greet contestants and guests at the airport and escort them to their organized transportation.
  • Metal detectors are in place at the contest venue entrance to ensure a safe and secure event for all attendees.
  • Access to ICPC events is restricted to registered guests.
  • ICPC Hotels, and the ICPC Operations Team have solid emergency and evacuation plans in place to ensure the safety and well-being of everyone involved.
