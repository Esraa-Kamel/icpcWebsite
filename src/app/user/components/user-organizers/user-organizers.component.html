Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AAST)

Established in 1972, the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AAST) has a rich history of providing top-notch education and training to students in the Arab world and beyond. Over the years, AAST has become a well-respected institution of higher education, with a reputation for academic excellence and a strong commitment to research and innovation. The university's founders envisioned AAST as a center of excellence that would help transform the Arab world through education, and today the institution remains committed to that mission. With its cutting-edge facilities, distinguished faculty, and robust academic programs, AAST is a vibrant and dynamic institution that is poised to continue making a significant impact in the years ahead for more information click here.

Ministry of Communications and Information Technology

The Egyptian Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) is the government body responsible for information and communications technology (ICT) issues in the Arab Republic of Egypt. The Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) launched a National Plan for ICT, establishing multi-disciplinary projects and initiatives related to vital sectors, such as education and health. The national plan helps the ministry promote the use of ICT nationwide as a means to achieve national development goals; and establish the pillars of a knowledge society in Egypt in close collaboration with other governmental, civil society and private sector entities click here.

Sharm El-Sheikh

Sharm El-Sheikh is an excellent choice for this prestigious event. The city has a long history of welcoming visitors from around the world, and its reputation for hospitality and professionalism is well-deserved. With its modern infrastructure, world-class hotels, and state-of-the-art convention center, Sharm El-Sheikh has everything needed to host an event of this magnitude. The city's location at the crossroads of Africa, Asia, and Europe also makes it an ideal location for international events, with easy access to major transportation hubs and a diverse range of cultural attractions. As the host of the ICPC 2023, Sharm El-Sheikh is committed to providing a safe, secure, and unforgettable experience for all participants, and looks forward to welcoming the world's best and brightest programmers to its shores.
