The Countdown Begins!

Get ready to mark your calendars for the 46th and 47th Annual ICPC World Finals, hosted by The Arab Academy of Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AASTMT), taking place in the picturesque city of Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt from 12-17 November 2023. This prestigious programming competition, sponsored by Huawei and JetBrains, will be a showcase of innovation and excellence, bringing together the brightest young minds in computer science to compete for the title of ICPC World Champion.
With the support of Institutional sponsors such as Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honor Society and the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology in Egypt, this event promises to be an exciting gathering of industry leaders, pushing competitors to their limits as they solve complex algorithmic problems. And, with its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality, Sharm El-Sheikh is the perfect backdrop for this unforgettable event. Don't miss out on this opportunity to witness history being made and be part of an adventure like no other.
