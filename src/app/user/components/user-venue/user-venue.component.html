Tonino Lamborghini International Convention Center Sharm El Sheikh:

The Arab Academy for Science, Technology, and Maritime Transport (AAST) has been chosen as the host of the 2023 ICPC World Finals Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt, and the prestigious event will take place at the Tonino Lamborghini International Convention Center Sharm El Sheikh. The convention center has been selected for its outstanding facilities and advanced technology.
