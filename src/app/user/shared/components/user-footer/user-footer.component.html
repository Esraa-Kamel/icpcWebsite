ICPC

The ICPC traces its roots to 1970 when the first competition was hosted by pioneers of the Alpha Chapter of the UPE Computer Science Honor Society. The initiative spread quickly within the United States and Canada as an innovative program to increase ambition, problem-solving aptitude, and opportunities for the strongest students in the field of computing.
